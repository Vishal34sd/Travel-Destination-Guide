Best Places to Visit

India Gate

A war memorial located in the heart of Delhi, surrounded by lush lawns and vibrant street activities.

Qutub Minar

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, this towering minaret is an architectural marvel of the Mughal era.

Red Fort

An iconic symbol of India, this historic fort showcases stunning Mughal architecture and rich history.

Lotus Temple

A Bahá'í House of Worship known for its unique lotus-shaped architecture and serene environment.

Humayun's Tomb

A magnificent garden tomb that served as an inspiration for the Taj Mahal.

Resorts and Hotels

The Leela Palace

A luxury hotel offering exquisite architecture, world-class amenities, and top-tier dining.

ITC Maurya

A premium hotel known for its grandeur and exceptional hospitality.

Taj Palace

An iconic hotel providing luxurious accommodations and a blend of traditional and modern charm.

Hyatt Regency Delhi

A contemporary hotel with stylish rooms, fine dining, and top-notch facilities.

Shangri-La Eros

A haven of luxury and comfort located in the heart of the city.

Famous Foods

Chole Bhature

A popular North Indian dish featuring spicy chickpeas and fluffy deep-fried bread.

Butter Chicken

A creamy and flavorful chicken curry that originated in Delhi.

Paranthas

Delicious stuffed flatbreads, often enjoyed with pickles and yogurt.

Kulfi

A rich and creamy frozen dessert, perfect for a hot day in Delhi.

Street Food

Indulge in golgappas, aloo tikki, and a variety of chaats available at every corner.

Famous Markets

Chandni Chowk

A bustling market known for its narrow lanes, street food, and diverse shopping options.

Sarojini Nagar

A budget-friendly market offering trendy clothing, accessories, and more.

Khan Market

An upscale market with premium stores, cafes, and bookstores.

Dilli Haat

An open-air market showcasing handicrafts, regional foods, and cultural performances.

Janpath Market

A vibrant market offering souvenirs, jewelry, and ethnic wear.
