Best Places to Visit

Taj Mahal

A UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the Seven Wonders of the World, this marble mausoleum is a symbol of love and architectural beauty.

Agra Fort

A historical fort that served as a residence for Mughal emperors, featuring stunning architecture and rich history.

Fatehpur Sikri

A deserted city built by Emperor Akbar, known for its well-preserved Mughal architecture.

Mehtab Bagh

A beautiful garden located opposite the Taj Mahal, offering stunning views, especially at sunset.

Itmad-ud-Daulah's Tomb

Often called the "Baby Taj," this exquisite tomb is a precursor to the Taj Mahal.

Resorts and Hotels

The Oberoi Amarvilas

A luxury hotel offering unparalleled views of the Taj Mahal and world-class amenities.

ITC Mughal

A premium hotel inspired by Mughal architecture, featuring lush gardens and excellent facilities.

Trident Agra

Known for its warm hospitality, beautiful rooms, and proximity to the Taj Mahal.

Hotel Clarks Shiraz

A heritage hotel offering comfortable stays with a blend of modern and traditional charm.

Courtyard by Marriott Agra

A contemporary hotel providing luxurious accommodations and top-notch services.

Famous Foods

Petha

A sweet delicacy made from ash gourd, available in various flavors and a must-try in Agra.

Bedai and Jalebi

A popular breakfast combo of spicy lentil-stuffed bread and sweet jalebis.

Dalmoth

A spicy and crunchy snack made with fried lentils and nuts, perfect as a souvenir.

Shahi Tukda

A rich dessert made with bread, milk, and dry fruits, showcasing Mughlai culinary influence.

Mughlai Cuisine

Enjoy flavorful dishes like biryani, kebabs, and curries that reflect Agra's royal heritage.

Famous Markets

Sadar Bazaar

A bustling market known for leather goods, handicrafts, and delicious street food.

Kinari Bazaar

A vibrant market offering jewelry, fabrics, and wedding-related items.

Shahganj Bazaar

Popular for household items, garments, and a variety of other products at reasonable prices.

Subhash Bazaar

Known for its silk products and a great place to shop for sarees.

TDI Mall

A modern shopping destination with branded stores, a multiplex, and food courts.
